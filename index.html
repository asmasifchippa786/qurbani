Eid-ul-Azha

EID DAY 1

First day of Eid-ul-Azha will be in Pakistan on 28th of june 2023 and According to Hijri calander will be 10th of Zil-hajj 1444.

EID DAY 2

Second day of Eid-ul-Azha will be in Pakistan on 29th of june 2023 and According to Hijri calander will be 11th of Zil-hajj 1444.

EID DAY 3

Third day of Eid-ul-Azha will be in Pakistan on 30th of june 2023 and According to Hijri calander will be 12th of Zil-hajj 1444.
